Experiment 1

If you would like to do this experiment you will need:

  • Petri dishes
  • Agar jelly
  • Cottons swabs
  • A cardboard box
  • Labels
  • A place to collect samples from

Top Tips!

If you have a chick incubator, you can use that in place of a cardboard box. [it helps to keep a consistent and monitored temperature.]

If you don't have petri dishes, use potato chip canister lids.

Tape the petri dish lids shut. [you are growing mold, it is best to not breath it in]

Label your petri dishes. When they have mold, it's hard to tell which is which.

Check on you petri dishes at least once, so if it's upside down, or the lid somhow opened, you can fix it.

Steps

  1. First, follow the directions on you agar jelly packet.
  2. Let the jelly cool for 1 minute, then pour into the petri dishes. Wait until solidified.
  3. Swab whatever you like, then rub the swab onto the petri dish.
  4. Close the petri dishes lid, and then put into a cardboard box, then wait for 15 or 10 days.
  5. Record your results, and process.
